Time to Roll!!!
Rules

- Each player has a cup that can hold up to 5 serves.
- The games plays in turns, which flow to the right of the player.
- During each turn a player can choose between 2 types of rolls (passive and agressive) or any of the 3 perks (Swap Frog, Revenge Gorilla or Uppps Unicorn) if available.
- The player drinks if his cup serve reaches 5 or if he gets a drink command (case in which he will only drink his current cup, not a full one).
